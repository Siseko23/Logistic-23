How container pricing works

1️⃣
You quote your rate

Submit your price per container per route, e.g. R20,000 for a 20ft from Durban to Johannesburg.

2️⃣
Platform adds 10%

FreightFlow adds a 10% platform fee. The shipper sees R22,000. You see your R20,000 only.

3️⃣
You file your invoice

After delivery, a PO is issued for your quoted rate. Upload your invoice against it to claim payment.

4️⃣
Paid your rate

Once verified, you receive exactly what you quoted — R20,000. Platform fee goes to FreightFlow.
