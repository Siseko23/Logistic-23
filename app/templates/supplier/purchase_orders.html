How to claim your payment

📋
1. PO is issued

The moment you accept a booking, a purchase order is created and available here. It contains all trip details, agreed rate, and our bank details.

🧾
2. Formulate your invoice

Use the PO details to create a tax invoice from your company. Include your VAT number, PO number, booking reference, and banking details.

📤
3. Upload your invoice

Upload your invoice as a PDF directly against the PO on this page. It goes straight to the FreightFlow finance team for processing.

💸
4. Get paid

Once your invoice is verified, payment is processed to your registered bank account. Payment terms are stated on each PO.
