How the AI engine works

  1. Filter suppliers by route match and vehicle availability
  2. Exclude suppliers outside the proximity radius
  3. Run truck matching engine — remove vehicles that can't carry the load
  4. Calculate composite score for each remaining supplier-vehicle pair
  5. Auto-select the highest-scoring supplier. If tied, price is tiebreaker
  6. Notify supplier via email + SMS. If rejected/timeout → auto-reassign to next
Auditability: Every AI selection logs the full composite score calculation per supplier — visible in the Bookings view for dispute resolution.
